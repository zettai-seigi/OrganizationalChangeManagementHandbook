# Chapter 10: Sponsorship and Leadership Engagement

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Define effective sponsorship roles and responsibilities
- Build sponsor coalitions
- Engage sponsors throughout the change lifecycle
- Measure sponsorship effectiveness

---

## The Sponsorship Model

### Sponsorship Roles

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| **Primary Sponsor** | Ultimate accountability, resources, decisions |
| **Sustaining Sponsors** | Direct reports of primary who cascade |
| **Reinforcing Sponsors** | Managers who reinforce with their teams |

### Sponsor Activities (ABC Model)

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Active** | Visible participation in change activities |
| **Build** | Coalition building with other leaders |
| **Communicate** | Direct messaging about the change |

---

## Key Takeaways

- Sponsorship is the #1 contributor to change success
- Sponsors must be active, visible, and consistent
- Coalition building extends sponsorship reach
- Sponsor engagement requires ongoing support

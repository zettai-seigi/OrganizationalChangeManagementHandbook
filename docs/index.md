## About This Handbook

The Organizational Change Management Handbook is a comprehensive guide designed for IT professionals, service management practitioners, and organizations implementing or optimizing ITIL/ITSM Organizational Change Management processes.

This handbook provides practical guidance, frameworks, and best practices for managing the people side of change, ensuring that individuals successfully adopt, utilize, and realize value from IT transformations.

### What You'll Learn

This handbook covers everything you need to know about Organizational Change Management:

- **Foundations** - Core concepts, ADKAR model, strategic framework, and critical success factors
- **Assessment & Planning** - Change readiness, stakeholder analysis, impact assessment, and OCM strategy
- **Execution** - Communication planning, training, sponsorship, and resistance management
- **Monitoring & Sustainment** - Adoption measurement, reinforcement, and maturity assessment
- **Governance** - Control objectives, policies, standards, and compliance
- **Implementation** - Roadmaps, best practices, common pitfalls, and practical guidance

### Who Should Read This

- **Change Managers** - Learn how to implement and optimize organizational change management
- **IT Leaders** - Understand integration with ITSM processes and IT transformations
- **Project Managers** - Design effective change strategies for project success
- **ITIL/ITSM Practitioners** - Deepen your organizational change management knowledge
- **HR Professionals** - Bridge organizational development with IT change initiatives
- **Students & Educators** - Comprehensive curriculum for training and certification

## Key Framework Elements

### The ADKAR Model

The foundational framework for understanding individual change:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **A**wareness | Understanding why change is needed |
| **D**esire | Personal motivation to support change |
| **K**nowledge | Understanding how to change |
| **A**bility | Skills and behaviors to implement change |
| **R**einforcement | Sustaining change over time |

### 8 Critical Success Factors

| # | Success Factor | Category |
|---|----------------|----------|
| 1 | Executive Sponsorship and Commitment | Leadership |
| 2 | Clear Vision and Case for Change | Foundation |
| 3 | Stakeholder Engagement and Involvement | People |
| 4 | Effective Communication | Communication |
| 5 | Training and Capability Building | Enablement |
| 6 | Change Agent Network | Structure |
| 7 | Resistance Management | Risk |
| 8 | Measurement and Reinforcement | Sustainability |

### 6 Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Target |
|-----|--------|
| Awareness Level | 100% |
| Adoption Rate | ≥85% |
| Proficiency Level | ≥80% |
| Resistance Level | ≤10% |
| Sponsor Engagement Score | Defined per initiative |
| Sustainment Rate | ≥90% |
